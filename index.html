About Me:

Hello! My name is Amber Kim. As you can see in my education and experience below, I took a bit of a round-a-bout way towards my journey as a Web Developer.

After graduating from the University of Washington, I moved with my husband to his next station duty in Camp Lejeune, NC. From there I tried to build a business doing photography and videography but it did not quite work because of all the deployments and relocation.

Before my second child was born and before my husband left the military, I was introduced by another military wife to working online for small businesses helping them remotely with whatever tech and marketing help they needed. Eventually I honed in to the tasks I really enjoyed which was building websites and web pages with WordPress using design tools but eventually I wanted to learn more and have more freedom to design websites from the ground up as a Full Stack Web Developer.
